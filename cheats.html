MUSIC PLAYER: Press M when the SPA3 logo passes the screen
NEW VS SCREEN: Before selecting VS from the main menu, press Space Bar whilst holding your cursor over         the spinning Polygon logo.
STAGE SELECT: On the title screen that says "Press Space to Start", hold down the Right Arrow, press Space           Bar and continue to hold the Right Arrow until you hear a laser sound-effect; now move onto the VS option         and hold in this order: LEFT, UP, SHIFT and CTRL. You will now get the stage select!
