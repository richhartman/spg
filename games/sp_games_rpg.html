South Park Games RPG
Evan Johnson

A blatant edit of Ultima's South Park Fantasy, South Park Games RPG somehow manages
 to take pre-existing engines and graphics... and make them WORSE. A short game with no sound and parts that are near-impossible to pass, South Park Games RPG is one to avoid. There are plenty of better South Park RPGs out there.
