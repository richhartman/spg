Scream in South Park
Subwoofer

One of the most influential South Park games ever made, this game started the mania of having games revolving around a storyline and featuring many gameplay types. In the South Park cover of the corny slasher movie, a killer is trying to murder the 4 boys and it's your job to save them and stop the killer in their tracks. Download this classic game in order to find out who the killer is!
