Serious Stan
SPGMike

The first game from SPGMike (of only two full games ever released, unfortunately), Serious Stan is a simple little platformer, obviously named after Serious Sam. When visitors kidnap and make clones of all his friends, Stan embarks on a mission 
 to destroy the clones, then bring down the aliens... tough job! Unfortunately, Serious Stan is plagued by some careless bugs and a rather average engine, though well worth a play if you enjoy platformers.
