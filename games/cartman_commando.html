Cartman Commando
Nomad

A Shoot 'em Up mission-type game courtesy of Nomad, where playing as Cartman, you must conquer six missions (one of which is a training mission) which involve assassinating Sally Struthers, saving Stan from Scuzzlebutt, Killing Damien in the playground, & capping Kathy Lee Gifford. Although small in comparison to other South Park shooting games, this is still a fun little title to enjoy. A great game at the time of its release.
