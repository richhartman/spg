Fantasy South Park
Beamer

A very early Beamer game, but one of his best for its time of release, Fantasy South Park is an interesting adventure game. When Kenny is sucked into his television, he enters 5 seperate fantasy worlds (Olden Days, Present, Jurrasic Era, Future and Black & White). In each world, he must battle various evil-doers and save the innocent, all by the aid of a wizard, Some Idiot Who Thinks He's Merlin. A charming game to play, and a good example of how gamers' skills improve over time.
