South Park Olympics 2004: Athens
Beamer

The first South Park Olympics game ever finished, South Park Olympics 2004: Athens was made in under 2 weeks. Playing as the four South Park boys, you play off against several nations in 8 different events: Running, Shooting, Swimming, Archery, Hurdles, Javelin, Diving and Weight Lifting. You can play each game individually in Trial mode (recommended for beginners), or play the game as a whole, with all 8 events in that order. Depending on how well you go, you stand to win either a Gold, Silver or Bronze medal, each worth different points. The main goal is to get the most points possible in the end... The controls change in each game change, as do the styles of gameplay. Earn a Gold medal in every event to win a special prize!
