South Park Starfighter
King of Darkness

Early, small game from King of Darkness, taking on a familiar concept with a South Park twist. When the four boys find an alien spaceship at Stark's Pond, they board it, and blast off into Hyperspace. However, they soon wind up on the opposite side of the universe without any booster engines! They have the co-ordinates to head home, but will need the booster engines on maximum to head home before fuel runs out. Can you guide them through a dangerous asteroid belt while their booster engines charge?
