Save South Park
Junkman

Although this is one of Junkman's earliest games, it is still regarded highly by many South Park Gamers. Save South Park involves Stan, Kyle, Kenny and Cartman sneaking out of a school field trip to a terrible movie, only to discover that after playing just outside of town, South Park has been taken over by a squad of evil, no-nonsense kids wielding guns. They then decide it is up to them to save the town, and set off on an adventure to bring the bad guys down, in a series of various levels, each with a different style of gameplay to offer. However, this proves to be no easy task, as you will learn when you encounter the game's challenging difficulty. Can you save South Park before it's too late?
