Butters Pro Skating
Beamer

A parody of the Tony Hawk's Pro Skater series, Butters Pro Skating was inspired heavily by Matt Kennedy's Stan Marsh Skateboarding. Using the Q, W, E and R keys for Advanced Tricks, A, S, D and F keys for Simple Tricks, and Z, X, C and V keys for Grinds, you can do a total of 12 tricks, each worth a different point value which drops as you do a trick more times. Unlock 9 different levels as you go, by completing any of the 5 goals possible. Between levels, compete in competitions to win medals which grant you access to more levels. Make Butters the next Professional skater today!
