South Ball Z
ML Productions

South Park Arcade meets Dragon Ball Z. Our favorite japanese weirdos are embarked into a struggling quest to beat up their foes in order to obtain South Balls. Well, that's how this reviewer see things... so don't complain if it's not right! An ambitious beat'em up game with a lot of levels and some cool features, such as a fireball powerup that can attack enemies from afar.
