Cartman Armageddon
Jamie Bailey

Cartman Armageddon is Jamie Bailey's graphical platformer, with a mix of both photo and shaded graphics. Kenny wants revenge for all his deaths, so he plans to destroy the world. Playing as Cartman, you must make your way through many dangerous territories to stop him.
