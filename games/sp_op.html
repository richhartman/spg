South Park: Omega Project
Stanster

What was going to be a simple comet sighting turns out to be Hell as Stan gets kidnapped by Evil Wendy, who can become incredibly powerful by kissing him. However, an obstacle stands in Evil Wendy's way: her nice self! Okay, the storyline is as typically corny and silly as they have ever been in Stanster's games, but luckly, the gameplay makes up for it in spades. You must guide Wendy through several levels packed with cultists galore: you have light cultists that are rather wimpy, yet are fast and wield knives, you got slower ones that shoot at you with pistols and finally scary-looking cultists with miniguns that can take a beating. Stanster's trademark thrilling boss battles are still present and will challenge even the most devoted action-platformer fan. Add to this the greatest original graphics ever seen in a South Park game and you got a game that you should download immediately.
