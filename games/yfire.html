Y-Fire
The Dude

The Dude debuts in South Park Gaming with a unique, top-down shooter based on X
 Fire. Y Fire is a very fun and advanced action game, playing out as a computerised version of Paintball. You must make your way through many different levels, essentially firing at anything that moves. However, Y Fire is much more tactical than it may sound, and it is without a doubt a challenging game that any South Park Game enthusiast will not want to miss.
