Future Stan
Beamer

Future Stan was an early attempt from Beamer at an epic game, which never recieved any feedback due to the fact that, when it was first released (Beamer's birthday waaayyy back in 2000), the file was corrupted. However, since then, he has managed to track down a fixed version, though he was too ashamed of the game to even consider re-releasing it. Despite this, Future Stan eventually saw the light of day again late in 2007. The game's storyline revolves around a gifted individual who can travel through time. Knowing the world will meet its demise in the future, 
 he travels back to 2000 where he locates Stan, and enlists his help in saving the world, by bringing alien refugees to Earth and helping them in an all-out war. There are a LOT of flaws in this game and one can see why Beamer would be ashamed of this title now. However, it is up there with South Park Espionage 2 as one of his better releases from 2000, and the nostalgia value is top-notch. Just try not to think about the totally nonsensical plot!
