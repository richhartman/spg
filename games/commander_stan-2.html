Commander Stan 2: Wendy's Revenge
Stanster

Stan might have managed to save all of his friends, but now, Damien and Satan is making him pay for his victory by trapping him in Hell to make up for his audacity! With Stan's blaster gun in Wendy's hands, she's the only one who can save the day. Many things have changed from the original game, although the core gameplay is still the same: gun down everything which moves toward you! However, even with the addition of new enemies and nasty boss battles with foes like Robo Stan, Evil Kyle and others, a really slow gameplay and a rather awkward engine plagues what could have been a great game. As it is, this is a game that has some sequences which sits right alongside the best moments of Commander Stan 1, yet is accompanied by rather boring parts, resulting in a rather disappointing sequel of a great game.
