Winpark 2
ML Productions

Released 2 years after the original, Winpark 2 was a real surprise when it came out - both in the sense that nobody saw it coming, and everyone was surprised at the game itself. Having nothing in common with the original Winpark apart from the storyline, the sequel is a behind-view action game. Ditching the espionage side of the game for all-out gun battles, it's debatable over which title in the series is better, although to be honest, this reviewer would be more inclined to recommend the first installment. Still, fans of mindless action games - if you can overlook the repetitive gameplay, you might get a kick out of this.
