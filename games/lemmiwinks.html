Lemmiwinks
Beamer

Beamer's only new release of 2007 is something that's been a long time coming in SPG - a game about Lemmiwinks, the Gerbil King. When the Frog Prince informs him that a great evil has taken over the animal kingdom, Lemmiwinks must once again dawn the helmut torch, in this difficult traditional platformer, heavily inspired by Sonic. Take on six different levels, ranging from a manic metropolis to haunted houses and collapsing caverns, dodging obstacles, avoiding traps and burning your enemies to get to the blue checkpoint at the end. Regarded as one of the hardest South Park games ever, though heavily criticised for its extreme difficulty and poor movement. Made for the 4th South Park Game-Making Contest.
