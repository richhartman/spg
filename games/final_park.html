Final Park
Kain X

KainX's final game, this is a RPG game straight from the Final Fantasy school of storytelling 
 (the game's story borrows a lot from Final Fantasy) with some rather weird twists. Despite an interesting (and underused) battle engine and a constantly present sense of humor, the game doesn't have a great deal of playability and it actually feels more like an interactive movie. Despite the game's rather degrading flaws, this remains a lot more interesting than many other people's creations. Just don't expect a lot of gameplay out of it.
