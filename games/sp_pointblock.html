South Park Point-Block
Beamer

An experimental arcade game from Beamer, South Park Pointblock is a mix of Pong, Breakout, and those "Put the balls in the clowns mouth" games at fairgrounds. However, it's just so hard to do that it's nearly unplayable. Worth checking out if you're into more original games, though.
