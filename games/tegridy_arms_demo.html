Tegridy Arms (Demo)
Beamer

A one-level alpha demo of an in-the-works first person shooter, Tegridy Arms marks a lot of firsts for SPG: Beamer's first new game in 13+ years, the first new SPG release of the 2020s, the first truly 3D SPG and the first SPG set in modern-era South Park (taking place during the Tegridy Farms arc, which spans season 22 onwards).
Intended as a throwback to old-school FPSs, Tegridy Arms puts you in the shoes of Randy Marsh, who must defend his weed farm when it comes under attack. Find the keys, progress through the gates, chuckle at the easter eggs and gun down anything that comes at you!
NOTE: Save/Load system currently broken. Upon death/level completion/settings changes, you will need to close the game entirely and restart.
