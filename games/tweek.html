Tweek
Andrew Rumney

An early platformer, Tweek vs the Underpants Gnomes (more commonly known as simply "Tweek") is based around everybody's favourite paranoid hyper kid... that's right, Bebe! ... I mean, Tweek! Going through a series of dangerous worlds, Tweek must re-collect all his underpants from the Underpants Gnomes, while defeating them with his trusty throwable cups of coffee. A simple (and somewhat repetitive) platformer obviously inspired by the Season 2 episode "Gnomes," Tweek 
 vs the Underpants Gnomes is perhaps Rumney's most successful game, and still has its share of fans today. Well worth a download.
