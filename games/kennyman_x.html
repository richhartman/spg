Best South Park - Mega Man X game? That might be a quite outrageous claim, especially coming from the game itself, but it's not too far away from the truth, especially since a great deal of Megaman related games in the SPG community were average at best. In this very short 2 level game, you get to shoot bullies with your usual arm cannon as well than a fart cannon which you collect in the first level. Very basic game, although it is fun to play.
