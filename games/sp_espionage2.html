South Park Espionage 2
Beamer

Originally regarded as Beamer's best game when it was released in 2000 (a title it lost the following year to Cyborg Kenny), South Park Espionage 2 takes place shortly after the original title. Mephisto's plans of world domination were stopped by Stan, and everything has gone back to normal. However, it appears that before his death, Mephisto created an evil, giant clone of Kenny called Kennarg to carry out his work. Kennarg then revives Mephisto's evil cloning operation, and moves to a hideout in Australia, where a computer program slowly blows up world cities. It is up to Stan, Kyle, Kenny, Wendy and Chef to go to Australia and stop him before South Park is destroyed. With 10 different levels, each 
 with different playable characters and different styles of gameplay to offer, South Park Espionage 2 is still one of Beamer's better titles, and a fun, interesting game worth checking out.
