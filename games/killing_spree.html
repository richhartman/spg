Killing Spree
Klaptrapx

A small and simple game made by Dark Yoshi Productions' member Klaptrapx, Killing
 Spree puts you in the shoes of a masked psycho, who, as you probably guessed
 from the title, goes on a killing spree. A few minutes of good, senseless violence
 that will undoubtedly please some people, but steer away if you're looking for
 a more substantial game.
