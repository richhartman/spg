South Park Soldiers: Snow Commando
Rodrinator

The third installment of the South Park Soldiers series brings a snow-based action-platformer, made by Marx Productions (known at the time as Rodrinator). Terrorist activities have been spotted near South Park, Colorado, and playing as Cartman, you are sent to sort things out. A very playable and enjoyable game, and a good example of the improvement in the creator's gaming skills, especially when compared to the first South Park Soldiers game. Made for the 1st South Park Game Making Contest.
