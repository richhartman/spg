South Park Arcade 3
Polygon

The bullies are on the rise again... which is really annoying Cartman, as he's the one who has to fight the bully opposition once more. However, he is not alone this time, as Stan, Kyle and Kenny will be joining him. In this all-time classic beat 'em up game, you need to play through 5 levels, which are populated by different types of bullies that can dish out various types of pain, as well as some challenging boss battles, pitting you against the likes of Phillip, Mr. Garrison, Damien, Mr. Hankey and finally... Mephisto. Pushing Klik 'n Play to it's outmost limits with colorful, scrolling stages that each faithfully portray places such as arenas, graveyards, funhouses and even a high-tech lab, and many fun powerups, this game is a blast to play. Each character even has his own unique stages, thus giving a good reason to come back to this game again. One of the best South Park games ever made, period.
