Cyborg Kenny
Beamer

Mephisto has just made a robotic clone of 8-year-old Kenny McCormick. However, it is
 pure evil, and after breaking out of the lab and killing all of Mephisto's guard
 clones, he begins a power struggle with his former master. Unfortunately, while
 Cyborg Kenny is harmed at the mere touch of a human, he is equipped with a handy
 built-in weapon, making life diffficult for all those trying to stop him! Playing
 as the pure evil Cyborg Kenny, venture all around the world from Roswell to
 Antarctica, searching for the 4 Omega Crystals. Upon gaining them, Cyborg Kenny
 will be able to make any wish he desires. Will Mephisto and his thousands of
 guard clones be able to stop him? Will Cyborg Kenny find the 4 Omega Crystals
 and be given great omega power? And what is it that Cyborg Kenny is looking
 for, anyway? Find out in this extremely violent SPG classic.
