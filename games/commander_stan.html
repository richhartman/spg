Stanster

Stan's friends have been kidnapped! To make matters worse, all hell has been brought loose upon South Park, as swarms of turkeys, aliens, zombies and even normal people have been cloned to wreak hhavoc over the city! Armed with a small, yet powerful blaster gun, it is your mission to guide Stan through levels of enemy-capping action, shooting through everything that stand in your way as well than many ferocious bosses such as a queen turkey, Mephisto, Damien and the notorious Evil Stan in this Stanster classic.
