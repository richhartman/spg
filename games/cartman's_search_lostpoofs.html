Cartman's Search for the Lost Cheesy Poofs
Scott Mahoney

Cartman is in dire need for cheesy poofs, yet for some reason, many clones of Kenny are guarding them! Using a selection of cheesy poof-like weapons (read pistol, shotgun, machinegun and bazooka), you must trash the Kenny clones and reach for the cheesy poofs in the game's levels. One of Scott Mahoney's earliest efforts, this is a basic, yet enjoyable platformer. Don't miss out on the Space Invaders-like level in the middle of the game, too.
