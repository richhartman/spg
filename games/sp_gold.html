South Park Gold
Mark Hadley

Another compilation game from Mark Hadley. Take down your old friends as Damien in Damien's Wrath, use Stan's football to save Thanksgiving in Turkey Terror, dodge barrels 
 as the boys to save their favourite things in Scuzzle-Kong, and take down UFO's with Cartman's rocks in Space Visitors. Some decent light entertainment, though far from Hadley's best.
