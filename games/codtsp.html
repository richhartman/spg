Come on Down to South Park
Beamer

A very original (and very early) South Park game. In Come on Down to South Park, you play as none other than the game's creator, Beamer, in which you move to South Park and befriend the four main boys. A choose-your-own-adventure game, Come on Down to South Park offers many fun little mini games, all tied together by a "point of view" story.
