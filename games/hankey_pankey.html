Hankey Pankey!
Beamer

Something of a spiritual (in more ways than one with the Christmas spirit) sequel to Beamer's prior puzzle game, Navigating Kenny, Hankey Pankey's goal has you, playing as Mr Hankey, traverse over all of the green squares without backtracking onto the red ones or it's instant game over!
