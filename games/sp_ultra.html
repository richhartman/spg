South Park Ultra
Mark Hadley

Another compilation game from Mark Hadley, and generally regarded as one of his better ones (though South Park Rage still reigns supreme). Help Mr. Hankey save Christmas, engage in an interesting little puzzle game in Chef's Chocolate Salty Balls, take down the boys as aliens in Visitation, create a violent bloodbath to save the kids in Zombies Ate My Cheesy Poofs, and race a second player to the triangle of Zinthar in Race for the Triangle. Some fun games in this compilation.
