Cocktails
RFI

A unique puzzle game from RFI in which you must keep Kenny juggling molotov cocktails for as long as possible without messing up. To do so, you need to vent bars that fall from the top of the screen through the hole on the bottom. When a bar touches the inner walls of the structure, it will stick - and if two bars collide, Kenny will mess up. However, reverse controls and bar rotation does NOT make things easy for you, neither do the bombs which fall from the ceiling. A little tricky to grasp at first, but very addictive once you get the hang of it.
