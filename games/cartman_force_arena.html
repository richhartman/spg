Cartman Force Arena
Junkman

An arcade version of the original game, Cartman Force Arena puts you in various levels from Cartman Force, pitting you up against various bad guys from there using weapons from the original game. However, Cartman Force Arena is very different from Cartman Force - while the original CF had you rescuing neighbours from the evil creatures and such, Arena simply has you gunning them down in their masses, testing your wits and weapon skills. The aim is to simply survive and earn points. How long can you last?
