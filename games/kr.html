Kenny's Rampage
Matt Kennedy

The grandfather of South Park Gaming's violent shooting spree games. After having been killed one too many times, it's time for Kenny to get even by killing everyone else! Even though it's a really basic game, you people who like violence might get a kick out of it, despite the utterly incompetent enemy AI. Its extreme violence made it a landmark South Park game back in 2000, and it is still a fondly-regarded title among many SPGers, despite the sequel surpassing it in many ways. Certainly one of the most important games in South Park Gaming history.
