Winpark
ML Productions

Based on WinBack: Covert Operations, WinPark: Stupid Operations was made during the phase of Espionage/Action games in 2000. Mainly focussing on the Espionage side of things, Winpark used rather simple AI for the guards to simulate when and when they wouldn't be able to spot you, forcing the player to sneak around challenging levels. Simple, but surprisingly good for such an early title. Can YOU stop the terrorists who have taken over South Park?
