Trapper Keeper Reloaded
Beamer

Arcade meets Platformer in the sequel to Beamer's first Trapper Keeper game, Trapper Keeper Reloaded. Playing as Kyle, you must go through several creepy areas, destroying the Cartman-Spawn that were created with the revival of Cartman and Trapper Keeper. Making your way to the Trapper Keeper, you earn points by collecting point tokens, destroying Cartman-Spawn and passing the levels as fast as possible. Can you destroy Trapper Keeper for a second time?
