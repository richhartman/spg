Alien Hunt
Dave

Alien Hunt is a small-but-sweet game with neat presentation, inspired by the old Nintendo game Duck Hunt. Using the mouse, you must take control of a crosshair and fire at visitors in their spaceships. Giving you a set amount of time and ammo to shoot down each ship, it's a fast paced beat-your-own score game that slowly increases in difficulty as it progresses. There are three different game types playable, including a practice round.
