Kenny's Deathmatch
Zpeef

The old saying goes: "People's first games are always bad". However, this is one of the rare exceptions. This is Zpeef's first and only game to be ever released to the South Park Gaming Community and the results are actually of a high caliber. Playing the part of Kenny, you are thrusted in the middle of deadly arenas where you need to fight multiple spawning robots to survive. Staying in one piece is a tough challenge, but with your smarts and wits, you can do it! Its graphics are small but very efficient, you'll never get lost or confused in the midst of the battlefield. The game's only real trouble is that it's rather 
 short, but the journey toward the game's ending is a thrilling and highly enjoyable 
 one. Download this game immediately.
