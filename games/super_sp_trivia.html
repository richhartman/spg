Super South Park Trivia
Beamer

The first full South Park game of 2005, a trivia game with over 250 questions about South Park. Test your South Park knowledge (of the first 8 seasons) in Easy, Medium and Hard modes, unlocking each as you go, in the game that will make even the most hardcore South Park fan scratch their head. Questions range from observational, to general fan knowledge and facts about the show. The tenth question is always a screenshot-spotting one. Get a perfect score in hard mode to unlock a special prize!

 NOTE: Some questions in this game have had incorrect answers set as their correct ones.
