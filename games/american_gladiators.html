American Gladiators
Nomad

Often name-checked as one of the top pioneers of the South Park Gaming community, this was Nomad's last release for the SPG scene before his departure. What he has left behind is the definitive sports game of the SPG scene. Themed around the popular American show American Gladiators, you must go through several events such as the Joust, the Wall, Powerball, Atlasphere and others. The game has a clean control scheme and has a really playful feel to it all thanks to Nomad's attention to detail, making the gameplay experience engrossing. Filled with dozens of secret unlockables and an always randomised game order, this is a game you won't get tired of. Recommended in a heartbeat.
