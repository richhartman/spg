Special Agent Kenny
BLUSPG

Joe Allen revisits Kenny's Rampage with his first game under the BLUSPG guise. This time, Kenny plays the part of a secret agent trying to stop terrorists from unleashing a nuclear strike. Even though the game is not quite variated and suffers from a silly plot, there is some fun to be found within this title thanks to it's slightly challenging gameplay.
