Cartman Kong Country
ML Productions

Cartman Kong Country is an obvious parody of the SNES classic Donkey Kong Country, in which the player must navigate Cartman Kong and Stan Kong through a series of levels. Collecting all the bananas, while stomping on (and throwing barrels at) some unfriendly critters known as the Kremlings, the game doesn't play exactly like the original Donkey Kong Country, but does try to replicate it on many levels (such as the graphics, which are taken directly from the game). A passable platform game, though nothing special.
