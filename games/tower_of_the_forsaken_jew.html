Tower of the Forsaken Jew
RFI & Jzayed

Jzayed teams up with another gamer once again - this time SPG veteran RFI - to put out a charming little platformer in an amazingly short amount of time. "Tower of the Forsaken Jew" places Kyle in a large, horrible tower he must scale and escape. Throughout five challenging levels, Kyle must dodge various obstacles and enemies, activate buttons, collect coins and avoid traps - all while racing against the clock for freedom! The first significant release of 2007.
