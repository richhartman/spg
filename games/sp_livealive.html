South Park Live a Live
Rodrinator

Based on the SNES game Live a Live, Marx Productions' "South Park Live a Live" is essentially a collection of 4 different games, or at least 4 different stories. It puts you in the shoes of the four main South Park boys, as you live a day in the life of them. Find funding for heaven with Kenny, discover why everyone's acting so strange with Stan, rescue Ike from Visitors with Kyle, and become a hero with Cartman. An interesting game full of variety, though not without its strange flaws. It was one of Marx Productions (A.K.A. Rodrinator)'s earlier projects, after all.
