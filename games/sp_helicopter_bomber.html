South Park Downball
Jzayed

Another small arcade-style game from Jzayed, South Park Downball is a computer version
of something we all did as children - bouncing a ball against a wall. Playing as Kenny, having the ability to move through an amount of pavement in front of
a wall, you must hit the ball against it to rack up as many points as you can.
However, lose the ball and you lose a life. The game ends when you have lost all
lives. A simple concept, and a fun (though very short) little game.
