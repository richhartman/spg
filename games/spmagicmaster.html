South Park and the Magic Master
Cartman434

One of the eldest SPGs made, and possibly the first of the RPG genre, this gem parodies Final Fantasy type games, featuring the boys seeking to stop an attack on the town by both UFOs and demonic monsters alike! Playing as Stan, it's your duty to put an end to the mayhem, meeting several familiar looking RPG characters along the way!

This game was lost from the face of the internet until it was unearthed by Zekium from the Kliktopia Discord Group. Zekium reportedly discovered the game while searching on an old CD-ROM. Many thanks, dude!  Until now, the only evidence that this game ever existed was this Internet Archive Listing and this obisidium forums Listing which leads back to the original Internet Archive listing.
