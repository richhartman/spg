Kenny's Quest
Link

An old game from 2000 (though regarded as a classic when it was released), Kenny's Quest is a simple, light-hearted platformer made by Link. The game puts you in the shoes of Kenny, who must make his way through numerous dangerous levels. Earning a life with each level he passes, Kenny must venture into space and save Stan, Kyle and Cartman, who have been kidnapped by aliens.
