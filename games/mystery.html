Mystery
Beamer

Originally made as a competition (won by Junkman), Beamer's game Mystery was a small project set in South Park Elementary. Playing as Stan, you had to scour the school for clues, to find out who killed Kenny. It would end with Officer Barbrady asking you to accuse a murderer. A small-but-fun game, original and somewhat well thought-out for an early project.
