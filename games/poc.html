Planet of Cartmans
Junkman

Planet of Cartmans is the fun, yet very challenging platformer that essentially put Junkman up on the list of great South Park Game Makers. After being kidnapped, Kenny finds himself part of a strange space project, then crash-lands on a planet inhabited by evil Cartmans set to kill. He then sets off on an adventure to discover the truth behind this strange planet... while violently kicking a large amount of Cartman ass, of course. Although the sequel definitely topped the original in every way, Planet of Cartmans is still an awesome game that everyone should experience. One of the most recognised games in the history of South Park Gaming.
