Golden Fatass
RFI

An early game released from RFI, back when he was known as Xplicit, in which you must play as secret agent Stan trying to hunt down Cartman. An unfortunately buggy and boring game, with many of its fonts (unintentionally) appearing in Wingdings - Golden Fatass is one to avoid.
