South Park Football
Andrew Rumney

Another of Rumney's early projects, which is set in the world of REAL football, or "soccer". This game is pretty much a rip-off of Polygon's South Park Ice Hockey Championship, only though there are only 2 possible matches to play through it. However, the right click shooting makes play a bit easier.
