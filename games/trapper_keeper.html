Trapper Keeper
Beamer

Trapper Keeper is an interesting Arcade game based on the South Park episode of the same name. Playing as Kyle, you must infiltrate Trapper Keeper and its various stages, armed with a gun. The goal is to bring down the core of Trapper Keeper, as in the episode, before it destroys South Park. However, Trapper Keeper won't make things easy for you - as in each stage are dozens of Cartman spider-creatures concealed within various bubbles. By bursting the bubbles, you will unleash them, and the goal is to destroy them before they either touch you or the ground. If they do so, you will lose a life. Will Kyle be able to save South Park, or will his fate be concealed inside the hybrid of a machine and a fat little boy? A unique and addictive arcade game, although gets somewhat repetitive after a while.
