Tribes of Parc
Jon Robson

An interesting RPG from Jon Robson, Tribes of Parc is a game in which 10 different tribes of South Park Gamers (and cannibals) are currently at war. Coming into it as a small tribe, you must battle the other tribes, convert as many to your team as possible, and become the ultimate Tribe of Parc! A flawed game that's full of in-jokes that only old community members will understand, Tribes of
 Parc may appeal to some RPG fans who particularly enjoy battles. Just don't expect too much from it.
