South Park Smash
Beamer

South Park Smash is the co-creation of Beamer and Python - an incredibly violent fighting game. Playing as Cartman and armed with a link of chain, you square off against Stan, Kyle and Kenny in a bloodbath tournament, held by the insane Ide. An interesting title, though very flawed gameplay-wise. Still, fans of gibs and gore will be satisfied - it's right up there with Kenny's Rampage as one of the most violent South Park Games ever made.
