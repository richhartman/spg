Cartman Leap
Jamie Bailey

In this platform game inspired by the series Quantum Leap, you play the part of Cartman, who's travelling back in time in order to prevent the destruction of South Park by a Chinese Shitmaster and his bouncing turds. A standard traditional platformer that is inhabited by great graphics as well than some action-based stages where you must fight the Shitmaster.
