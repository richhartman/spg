South Park Fland Fish
Beamer

Released in disguise as a South Park Mafia demo, South Park Fland Fish was Beamer's April Fools joke of 2005. The most boring game of all time, its repetitive gameplay only gets more and more annoying as the game continues. There is no storyline, there is no reason for its existence, and the whole joke is that there's no joke to it. May be funny to some... Oh, and I have no idea what the hell a Fland Fish is.
