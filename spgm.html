The South Park Gaming Magazine Page

You knew a group of kids making South Park fan games had issues!If you grew up around the same time most South Park Gamers did, you'd remember gaming magazines, and quite likely would've owned some - print publications with a mix of game reviews, game previews, articles, thinkpieces, and quite often a demo disc (which would nowadays take the form of digital publications such as IGN and Kotaku). This was the SPG community's attempt to create their own version of such magazines - a downloadable collection of all the above, released on a monthly (then later bi-monthly) basis.

There were two separate publications:
South Park Gaming Magazine, which ran for 7 issues between January 2001 and July 2001.
South Park Gaming Bi-Monthly, which ran for 4 issues between January 2006 and June 2006.
Both were helmed by Beamer, with other contributors credited therein.
